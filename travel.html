Everything you need to know about reaching us in Mexico

CDMX

Mexico City International Airport

Guests are advised to fly into Mexico City International Airport. Private transport will be waiting to take you directly to your hotel, ready for the celebrations.

Flying Direct to Mexico City

The most straightforward option is a direct flight into Mexico City International Airport (CDMX). From arrival, private transport will be on hand at any time to take you straight to your hotel — no need to arrange anything yourself.

Via Cancún — Extend Your Trip

For those wishing to extend their trip, you may choose to fly via Cancún and enjoy a holiday beforehand. We will ourselves be flying from Cancún to Mexico City a few days before the wedding — you're welcome to join us on that journey.

Weekend Transport

Throughout the entire wedding weekend, transport will be arranged between both hotels and Hacienda Acamilpa. Guests will be collected on Saturday between 12:30 – 1:00 PM, with transport available to and from the venue at all times across all three days.

Concierge Service

A dedicated concierge and driver service will be available throughout the weekend. Driver details will be shared in advance. For any assistance — from airport transfers to last-minute requests — we are here for you.
